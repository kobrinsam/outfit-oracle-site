Privacy Policy

Outfit Oracle — Last updated April 5, 2026

Outfit Oracle ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used.

Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data required to provide app functionality:

  • Location (approximate): Used once per session to retrieve your local weather so outfit recommendations match today's conditions. Location is not stored by us and is not used for any other purpose.
  • Photos: If you choose to add a photo to a wardrobe item, that image is stored locally on your device only. It is never uploaded to our servers.
  • Wardrobe & Settings Data: Your clothing items, wear history, and preferences are stored locally on your device using device storage. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Outfit Oracle uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenWeather: Your approximate location (latitude/longitude) is sent to OpenWeather to retrieve current weather conditions. No personally identifying information is included. See their privacy policy.
  • OpenAI (via our secure proxy): Your wardrobe item list (names and colors) and the current weather are sent to an AI model to generate outfit recommendations. No personally identifying information is included. Requests pass through our own Cloud Function; your data is not stored by us after the request completes. See OpenAI's privacy policy.
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect a device identifier for purchase validation purposes. See their privacy policy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with any third party beyond what is described above and what is strictly necessary to operate the app.

Data Retention

All wardrobe data, wear history, and settings are stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data. We do not retain any user data on our servers.

Children's Privacy

Outfit Oracle does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us at support@outfitoracle.app.
