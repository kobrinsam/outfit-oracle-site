What it does

Outfit Oracle recommends what to wear each day based on your local weather and what's actually clean in your closet. Add your clothes once, then let the AI do the rest.

AI Outfit Picks

Get a complete, weather-appropriate outfit suggestion in seconds — guided by your style preferences.

Wardrobe Tracking

Catalog your clothes with photos, colors, and types. Know what you own at a glance.

Clean & Dirty Status

Track wear counts per item. Set custom limits per clothing type. One-tap laundry to reset everything.

Live Weather

See today's conditions right in the app so every recommendation fits the forecast.
